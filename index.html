Our Story

A Beach That Belongs to the Neighborhood

Kiddie Beach is a beloved community beach tucked away at the tip of Gerritsen Beach — a tight-knit Brooklyn peninsula between Marine Park and Sheepshead Bay. For generations, local families have gathered here every summer to give their kids a safe, fun-filled place to swim, play, and grow up together.

Accessible from the ends of Hyman Court and Post Court, our private beach is maintained and supported by the community through annual memberships. More than just a beach — it's where neighborhood kids learn to swim, make lifelong friends, and create the kind of summer memories that stay with them forever.

Summer Fun

What We Offer the Kids

From sunrise to sunset, there's always something happening at Kiddie Beach. Our summer programs keep kids active, social, and off screens all season long.

🏊

Swimming & Water Play

Supervised swimming in our safe, monitored beach waters. From beginners wading in the shallows to confident swimmers — every kid belongs in the water here.

🏀

Basketball & Sports

Pickup basketball games, organized tournaments, and all-day sports keep kids competitive and active. Our courts stay packed all summer long.

🎤

Karaoke & Open Mic

Summer nights come alive with karaoke, open mic events, and community entertainment. Kids and families get on stage and make some noise.

🎉

Community Events

Fundraisers, cookouts, holiday celebrations, and special events bring the whole neighborhood together. There's always something to look forward to.

🎯

Dart League

Adults get in on the action too. The Kiddie Beach dart league brings friendly competition and bragging rights all summer long.

🏇

Horseshoes

A Kiddie Beach classic. Grab a partner, step up to the pit, and toss some ringers. Horseshoes keeps the adults entertained while the kids play.

A Day at Kiddie Beach

Typical Summer Day

Here's what a full day at the beach looks like for our kids

Morning

Open Swim & Free Play

Kids arrive, set up on the sand, and hit the water. Supervised swimming, sandcastle building, and morning games get the day started right.

Late Morning

Organized Sports & Activities

Basketball games, organized sports, and group activities get the energy up. Something for every age and interest level.

Afternoon

Snack Bar & Hangout

Refuel at the snack stand, hang with friends, or just soak up the sun. The beach is the neighborhood living room in the summer.

Evening

Community Events & Entertainment

As the sun goes down, the fun shifts to karaoke nights, open mic sessions, fundraisers, and family cookouts under the Brooklyn sky.
