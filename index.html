— Why Kiddie Beach

Real ball, real summer.

A Brooklyn institution for over 30 years. Our mission is simple — keep kids active, off the couch, and part of something bigger than a screen. Every summer, every court, every kid.

01 / TRADITION

A Brooklyn tradition

Run by neighbors, for neighbors. Three decades of Gerritsen Beach kids — and now their kids — coming up through this league.

02 / EVERYONE

Ages 3 to 16

Clinics for the little ones and competitive teams for older kids. Nobody sits the bench — everyone plays.

03 / OPEN

Boys & girls welcome

Mixed teams plus girls-only games available. Whatever your kid is into, there's a spot for them.

04 / SCHEDULE

Built for parents

Weekday games at 6 PM and Sundays at 5 PM — convenient times that don't blow up the family calendar.

05 / CLINICS

Saturday clinics

Saturday morning skill sessions designed for our youngest players, ages 3 – 6. First dribbles, first shots, first wins.

06 / PLAYOFFS

Playoffs & awards

Every kid gets a real season with a real ending — playoff brackets, trophies, and a final-day celebration.
